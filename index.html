    Musselman Oilfield Services

    Well-site Consulting Services




  • About

    Musselman Oilfield Services, LLC is a fully insured company providing specialized well-site consulting which includes, but is not limited to: completions, workovers, facility and pipeline installation, dirt construction, plug and abandoning, and landowner relations. Through the numerous vendors our company has done business with in the past, we are able to obtain bids for specific jobs and required services, equipment, pipe and fittings.

    Customer service is our first priority, therefore, we strive to effectively identify and serve your individual needs. The goal of Musselman Oilfield Services is to provide service in a safe, efficient and cost-effective manner.
